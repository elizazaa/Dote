Here is this month's list of restaurants that we recommend!

Claudine Restaurant

Located in a beautifully preserved colonial chapel amid the lush greenery of Dempsey Hill, Claudine offers a romantic escape from city life. With stained glass windows, plush velvet banquettes, soft greens and pinks, and soaring ceilings, the ambiance is enchanting. Chef Julien Royer, co-owner of the three-Michelin-starred Odette, brings classic French comfort cuisine to the table. Start with the exceptional homemade sourdough and irresistible Iberico butter. For mains, enjoy the Vol-Au-Vent, a flaky puff pastry filled with creamy veal sweetbreads and earthy morel mushrooms, or the Claudine Bouillabaisse, a rich and hearty fish stew from Provence featuring carabinero prawns.
39C Harding Rd, Singapore 249541
Make a reservation!

La Brasserie

La Brasserie's floor-to-ceiling windows offer stunning views of the Marina Bay waterfront, perfect for a romantic evening. The plush interiors combine modern elegance with classic European charm, creating an inviting ambiance. The menu features French bistro fare with three-course ($78) or four-course ($98) set dinners. Begin with options like Angus beef tartare, foie gras, or a seafood bouillabaisse with sea bass and crab. For mains, savor the richness of the cote de boeuf, a beef ribeye, or try the succulent Lumina lamb with black garlic, sherry, and shallots. Another standout is the toothfish in a creamy anchovy carrot veloute. Conclude with a sweet apple tarte tatin or a classic creme brulee with vanilla gelato.
39C Harding Rd, Singapore 249541
Make a reservation!

PS Cafe Harding

PS.Cafe at Harding Road, a pioneer in Dempsey Hill since 2005, offers a serene escape with panoramic views of lush greenery through its floor-to-ceiling windows. Known for its modern, spacious design, this location features a menu of classic PS.Cafe dishes alongside exclusive offerings. The full dessert counter boasts an expanded selection of indulgent cakes and pastries.
39C Harding Rd, Singapore 249541
